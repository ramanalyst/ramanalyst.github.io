I am Ramia, a socioeconomic researcher with over 20 years of experience coordinating and conducting diverse research projects for national and international institutions. Throughout my career, my work has focused on socioeconomic policies, social studies, institutional economics, and sustainable development. Over these two decades, I developed deep expertise in survey design, impact assessment, and the core logic of data management.

As a natural evolutionary step in my career, I have expanded and modernized my technical toolkit to transition into freelance data analytics. To meet the demands of modern data tasks, I have developed strong, practical skills in advanced programming and database management. Leveraging Python and MySQL for robust data engineering, I now clean messy data streams and transform complex research metrics into intuitive, interactive dashboards using Tableau, Power BI, and Excel.

By combining 20 years of deep analytical research background with modern data science tools, I offer a unique perspective. Whether you need an academic research partner or a commercial data pipeline analyst, I deliver clean, structured data and actionable insights.

I am fully available for strategic consultations, short-term projects, and full- or part-time contract roles.
